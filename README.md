# Seattle Weekend Guide

A beautiful, interactive web app to help your cousins find the perfect things to do in Seattle based on their personality and interests.

## Features

✨ **Interactive Filtering** - Choose between Introvert, Extrovert, or Both vibes  
🎨 **Gorgeous Design** - Modern, responsive layout with smooth animations  
📍 **18+ Curated Activities** - Museums, parks, neighborhoods, food, and more  
🔗 **Direct Links** - Quick access to websites and booking info  
📱 **Mobile Friendly** - Perfect on phones, tablets, and desktop  

## Your Cousins

👩‍🏫 **Teacher & Extrovert (58)** - Loves social events, meeting people, trying new restaurants  
👩‍💼 **Social Worker & Introvert (24)** - Passionate about arts, quiet museums, meaningful conversations  

## Activities Included

- Pike Place Market
- Seattle Art Museum (SAM)
- Chihuly Garden and Glass
- Capitol Hill & Fremont (vibrant neighborhoods)
- Discovery Park & Volunteer Park
- Kerry Park Viewpoint
- Seattle Central Library
- Museum of Pop Culture
- Green Lake Walk
- Ballard Breweries
- Mount Rainier Day Trip
- Coffee Culture
- And more!

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a browser
2. Click filter buttons to explore by vibe
3. Click "Learn More" to visit activity websites
4. Share the link with your cousins!

## Hosting

This is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. Just push to the `main` branch and enable Pages in repo settings.
